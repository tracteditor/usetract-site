How it works

Three questions.
One source of truth.

01

What did they promise?

Campaign statements, debate quotes, and public commitments — documented and sourced before they took office.

02

What did they actually do?

Every vote, sponsored bill, committee seat, and attendance record — pulled directly from GovTrack and Congress.gov.

03

Who is paying them?

Donor industries cross-referenced with committee seats and votes — the chain made visible from OpenSecrets and FEC filings.

04

You decide what it means.

No editorial scoring. No political agenda. TRACT shows you the record. The conclusion is yours.

Our mission

"See if your elected officials are doing what they promised — no spin, just the record."

TRACT exists to inform voters — not influence them. The same methodology applies to every politician regardless of party. A Republican and a Democrat are held to the identical standard. The data speaks. You listen.
